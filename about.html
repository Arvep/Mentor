About Us

EduSphere: Future Education

EduSphere aims at advancing your personal development and career perspectives by introducing you to the soft skills you need to excel in your private and professional life.

  • Your learning experience with edusphere is shaped by you! You can choose the coach that fits you best by taking into consideration your personal journeys and background.
  • Our coaches bring along a diverse set of qualifications, work and life experiences that make the coaching impactful and a unique, engaging experience.
  • EduSphere combines a local in-person coaching with a virtual component that allows you to access coaches from wherever you are and whenever you want.
